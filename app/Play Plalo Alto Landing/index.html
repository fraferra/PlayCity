What Is Play Palo Alto?



Through the application of game based thinking and game design techniques we aim to increase the level of civic engagement across the residents and visitors of Palo Alto, CA.

The main idea is this: As a user, you can choose to take on challenges and volunteering events through which you help out the city. After completion you will earn points that can be converted into discounts and prizes redeemable at local shops and businesses. Not only are we rewarding you for your hard work, we’re also helping boost the local and independent economy of Palo Alto. Sounds fun, doesn’t it?

Easy Navigation

Browse events and challenges, complete the tasks and enjoy the rewards from your earned points. See the level of civic engangment across the city of Palo Alto and compete your fellow citizens in a real game. Save your favorite events or challenges and enjoy!

Track Your Civic Engagement

The Profile Page enables users to monitor their points at any time. Furthermore an Experience counter will project the civic engagement of every registered citizen, creating a community leaderboard where the winner of a specific period will be rewarded.
